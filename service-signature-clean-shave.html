Signature Clean Shave (45 Min) — AED 100

Our Signature Clean Shave is the ultimate premium shaving experience. Over 45 minutes, your barber takes you through a full hot and cold towel ritual combined with our finest shaving products for a close, smooth, and deeply refreshing shave. This is classic barbershop luxury delivered to your home.

The 45-minute session allows time for a thorough hot towel preparation, a multi-pass straight-razor shave with premium shaving cream, face massage, and a cold towel closing treatment. We finish with a premium after-shave balm to leave your skin soothed, moisturised, and glowing. The Signature Clean Shave is perfect before a special event or as a regular treat.

What to Expect

A full luxury shaving ritual with hot and cold towel treatment, premium products, and a precision straight-razor finish — all at your location in Dubai.

How is the Signature Clean Shave different from the standard clean shave?

The Signature Clean Shave includes a longer session (45 min), a multi-pass razor technique, a face massage, and premium post-shave skincare. It is a full grooming ritual compared to the quicker standard clean shave.

What premium products are used in this service?

We use professional shaving creams, pre-shave oils, hot and cold towels, a precision straight razor, and premium after-shave balm. All products are selected for performance and skin safety.

Is this service suitable as a pre-event grooming session?

Absolutely. The Signature Clean Shave is ideal before weddings, business events, or important occasions. The result is a polished, photo-ready look that lasts throughout the day.
