Hairstyling (20 Min) — AED 75

Want a polished finish without a full cut? Our Hairstyling service gives you a fresh, styled look using premium products selected for your hair type. In 20 minutes our barbers blow-dry, shape, and finish your hair for an event-ready result that holds all day.

Our barbers use professional-grade styling products including pomades, clays, and light-hold creams sourced from trusted grooming brands. We assess your hair texture and preferred finish — from matte to shine — before recommending the best product for lasting hold throughout your day.

What to Expect

A quick yet polished styling session that leaves you looking event-ready — using premium products that hold all day.

Can I add hairstyling onto a haircut appointment?

Yes. Hairstyling is often combined with a haircut or skin fade for a complete finish. Let us know when booking and we will schedule the additional time.

What products do you use for styling?

We use a selection of professional pomades, hair clays, matte pastes, and light waxes from trusted grooming brands. We will recommend a product based on your hair texture and desired finish.

Is the 20-minute service enough for a full restyle?

The 20-minute service is ideal for refreshing and finishing an existing haircut. For a full restyle or colour-treated hair, we recommend booking a haircut along with the styling add-on.
