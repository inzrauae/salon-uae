Haircut - Skin Fade (45 Min) — AED 99

Our signature skin fade is the most popular haircut at Fade & Blade. In 45 minutes, our expert barbers deliver a flawless blend from skin to your chosen length — crisp detailing, sharp edges, and a modern finish that holds its shape all week.

A skin fade requires precision technique and the right tools. Our barbers use professional-grade clippers with multiple guard sizes to create seamless gradients from zero up to your desired length. Whether you prefer a low, mid, or high fade, we ensure sharp lines and a clean neckline every time.

What to Expect

A precision skin fade with sharp detailing and a contemporary finish — delivered to your door across Dubai.

What is the difference between a low, mid, and high fade?

A low fade starts just above the ear, a mid fade begins around the temple, and a high fade sits near the top of the head. Our barbers will recommend the best option based on your head shape and personal style.

How long does a skin fade last before needing a touch-up?

Most skin fades start showing regrowth after 2–3 weeks. For a consistently sharp look, we recommend booking a fresh fade every 3–4 weeks.

Can I combine a skin fade with beard styling?

Yes. You can add a Beard Grooming or Signature Beard service to your skin fade appointment. Just let us know when booking and we will schedule the combined time accordingly.
