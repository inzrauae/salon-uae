Signature Beard (45 Min) — AED 100

Elevate your beard game with our Signature Beard service — a comprehensive 45-minute grooming session designed to deliver a refined, polished beard finish. From detailed line-up and shape to conditioning treatment and styling, this is the full-spectrum beard experience for the discerning gentleman.

The Signature Beard session begins with a consultation to understand your beard goals and face structure. Our barber then performs a precision trim and shape, detailed line-up using a straight razor, hot towel softening treatment, beard oil or balm conditioning, and final styling for a sharp and intentional finish. Every element is crafted to ensure your beard looks its absolute best.

What to Expect

A premium 45-minute beard grooming ritual including shape, line-up, conditioning, and styling — delivered to your door across Dubai.

What does the Signature Beard include that a standard beard does not?

The Signature Beard adds a detailed razor line-up, hot towel treatment, beard oil or balm conditioning, and precision styling on top of the standard shape and trim — all in a relaxed 45-minute session.

Can the Signature Beard be combined with a haircut?

Yes, and it is our most recommended combination. The Signature Beard pairs perfectly with our Haircut or Skin Fade service for a complete head-to-beard grooming experience. You can also find this combination in our monthly Signature Package.

What beard conditioning products are used?

We use premium beard oils and balms from trusted professional grooming brands. The product used is selected based on your beard type, length, and skin condition to ensure optimal nourishment and a healthy, well-groomed appearance.
